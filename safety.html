Safety Centre

Kronos — Your Time is Currency

Before You Meet

  • Always meet in public first. Choose busy, well-lit locations — a café, library, or community centre — for your first exchange.
  • Tell someone where you're going. Share your meeting details with a trusted contact.
  • Trust your instincts. If something feels wrong, it's okay to cancel.

Verified Profiles

Verified users have signed in with Apple ID and passed our age verification. Look for the ✓ Verified badge. Always prefer verified users for exchanges.

Reporting

If a user makes you feel unsafe, harasses you, or violates our Community Pledge:

  1. Open the user's profile
  2. Tap Report a User
  3. Select the reason and add details
  4. Tap Submit

We take all reports seriously. Users who violate our policies will be suspended or permanently banned.

Blocking

You can block any user from their profile. Blocked users cannot contact you or see your profile.

Categories We Don't Allow

For your safety, Kronos does not permit offers in healthcare, childcare, elderly care, or any service requiring professional licensing we cannot verify.

Emergency

If you are in immediate danger, call your local emergency number. In Australia dial 000, in the UK dial 999, in the EU dial 112, in the US dial 911.

Contact

To report a safety concern: safety@kronos.app
