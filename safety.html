Safety Centre Kronos — Your Time is Currency Your safety is our highest priority. This page explains how Kronos keeps you safe and what to do if something goes wrong. Before You Meet Always meet in public first. Choose busy, well-lit public locations — a café, library, or community centre — for your first exchange with any new person. Tell someone where you're going. Use the Share Plan feature in the app to send your meeting details to a trusted contact. Trust your instincts. If something feels wrong, it's okay to cancel. The Safety Checklist Before every trade, Kronos prompts you to confirm: You have chosen a public meeting place You have shared your plan with someone you trust You have a way to contact emergency services You are meeting someone with a verified profile Check-In Timer Kronos includes a Check-In Timer. Set a duration before your meeting — if you don't check in when the timer ends, the app will prompt you and optionally alert your emergency contact. Verified Profiles Verified users have signed in with Apple ID and passed our age verification. Look for the ✓ Verified badge on profiles. Always prefer verified users for exchanges. Reporting If a user makes you feel unsafe, harasses you, or violates our Community Pledge: Open the user's profile Tap Report a User Select the reason and add any details Tap Submit We take all reports seriously. Users who violate our policies will be suspended or permanently banned. Blocking You can block any user from their profile. Blocked users cannot contact you or see your profile. Categories We Don't Allow For your safety, Kronos does not permit offers in the following categories: Healthcare, medical, or clinical services Childcare or babysitting Elderly care Any service requiring professional licensing we cannot verify Emergency If you are in immediate danger, call your local emergency number. In the UK dial 999, in the EU dial 112, in the US dial 911.
