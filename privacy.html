Privacy Policy Kronos — Your Time is Currency Last updated: April 2026 Overview Kronos is built on a privacy-first, decentralised identity model. We collect as little data as possible and store almost none of it on our servers — because we don't have any. What We Collect Identity: Your identity is verified by Apple (Sign in with Apple). We receive only a persistent, anonymous User ID from Apple. We never receive your name, email address, or Apple ID credentials unless you explicitly choose to share them. No profile database: We do not maintain a central database of user profiles. No passwords: Kronos has no password system. Authentication is handled entirely by Apple. What Stays on Your Device The following data is stored only on your device and never transmitted to us: Your anonymous User ID (stored in the iOS Keychain) Your time-credit balance and transaction history Your display name and skills list Your Trust Token (used for Bluetooth handshakes) Bluetooth & Location Kronos uses Bluetooth to discover nearby users. This requires Location permission on iOS. We do not track, store, or transmit your location. Peer connections are made directly between devices — no data passes through our servers. Data Sharing We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party. Data Deletion You can delete all locally stored data at any time by deleting the app from your device. To revoke Apple ID access, go to: Settings → [Your Name] → Password & Security → Apps Using Apple ID → Kronos → Stop Using Apple ID. Children Kronos is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
